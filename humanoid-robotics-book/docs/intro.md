# Humanoid Robotics & Physical AI

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Humanoid Robotics and Physical AI. This book will take you through the fundamentals of embodied intelligence and guide you in building autonomous humanoid robots.

This book is structured into four main parts:

### Part 1: Foundations of Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics
- Understanding Physical AI
- Humanoid Robot fundamentals
- Why Embodied Intelligence matters

### Part 2: Module-Based Learning
- **ROS 2 Basics**: Learn the Robot Operating System
- **Digital Twin**: Simulation and modeling
- **Isaac Platform**: NVIDIA's robotics simulation
- **VLA Models**: Vision-Language-Action models

### Part 3: Capstone Project
- Building a complete autonomous humanoid system
- Integration patterns and best practices
- System architecture

### Part 4: Future of Humanoid Robotics
- Emerging trends and technologies
- Career paths in robotics
